High DPI/4K Support

Unicode and Localization



GTK uses UTF-8 encoding for all of the text. This ensures that you can develop the app in your native language or any other language around the world.
{{ 'movement' | upcase }}

Gestures



In GTK, pointer and touch events are handled indistinctly, making your apps work on touchscreens out of the box.
{{ 'performance' | upcase }}

Hardware Accelerated Rendering



The internal logic of the Vulkan renderer has been reworked to use textures instead of cairo surfaces for intermediate results and thus avoid more cairo fallbacks.
